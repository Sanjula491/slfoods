Traditional Recipes

Discover a collection of traditional Sri Lankan recipes that have been passed down through generations. From aromatic curries to delectable sweets, each recipe tells a story of Sri Lanka's vibrant culinary heritage.

Kottu Roti

Kottu Roti is a popular Sri Lankan street food made with chopped roti, vegetables, and your choice of meat or tofu. It's a flavorful and satisfying dish that's perfect for any time of day.
